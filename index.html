About Us

Phipps Conservatory and Botanical Gardens, located in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, is a historical greenhouse and botanical garden. Founded in 1893 by steel and real estate magnate Henry Phipps as a gift to the city of Pittsburgh. We are located in Schenley Park on a span of 15 acres, offering a 14 room glasshouse and various outdoor gardens.

NOTABLE FEATURES: The Palm Court: This is located upon entrance, our Victorian-style glasshouse filled with stunning palms Tropical Forest Conservatory: One of most immersive exhibits, make your way through many themes such as Indonesia, Cuba, and Thailand. Center for Sustainable Landscapes: If you love the color green this is the place for you! One of the greenest buildings in the world, producing more energy than it consumes.
