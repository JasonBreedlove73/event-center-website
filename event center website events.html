Come and enjoy our Bonsai Exhibit

Jan.11-March 9

Bonsai, an ancient practice originating from china and refined in Japan. This exhibit introduces an exotic twist, featuring species that thrive in warm,humid environments. Journey through our curated collection of bonsai in the Serpentine Room inspired by Japanese culture. Visitors to this exhibit can marvel at an impressive array of species. See how many you can pick out! The Tropical Bonsai Exhibit is designed as an immersive experience, guiding visitors through winding pathways. Unlike staic sculptured, bonsai is living art that changes over time. Experiance seasonal transformations with growth in the spring, vibrant blooms in the summer, and changes in leaf colors during the cooler months.

An Invitation for Reflection: Bonsai mimic trees that bear the marks of forces like wind and rain. Their beauty lies in these characteristics that come with time. Is there a Bonsai in this room that especially resonates with you? Come and find out!



Enjoy our orchids in the Sunken Garden

March 10-April 15

Pink, Maroon, Yellow, White, Blue, and Purple! Our orchids shine bright among the dazzling water sequence in the Victoria Room. Our Orchid Exhibit is like entering a dreamscape of color and fragrance. With thousands of species and hybrids on display, this exhibit showcases one of the most diverse and enchanting plant families on Earth! With this exhibit visitors will experience an immersive journey through a lush greenhouse where our orchids flourish. Come in and experience the different smells, some sweet and floral, others rich and spicy, a few will surprize you with aromas like chocolate or vanilla.



Frolic with the Ferns!!

April 16-May 10

Check out our Fern Room where we have tropical and subtropical ferns. Step into prehistoric paradise where lush greenery streches in every direction. Visitors are guided through a winding path where ferns grow in abundance, creating a cool, tranquil atmosphere. Each species in the exhibit is dispayed in a nauralistic setting, allowing visitors to see how these plants adapt to different environments. Our exhibit is more than just a collection of plants, its a journey through time. The Fern Exhibit is a mesmerizing journey into one of the oldest plant groups on Earth. Ferns have thrived for over 360 million years, predating the dinosaurs! So come on out and pay respect to your elder!
